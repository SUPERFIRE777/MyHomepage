積分002

以下の不定積分を求めよ。

$$\int \frac{\ln \sin x}{\tan x} \dd x$$
解答 $$ \begin{aligned} \int \frac{\ln \sin x}{\tan x} \dd x &= \int \frac{\ln \sin x}{\frac{\sin x}{\cos x}} \dd x \\ &= \int \frac{\ln \sin x}{\sin x} \cdot \cos x \dd x \end{aligned} $$ ここで、\(\sin x = t\)とおくと、\(\cos x \dd x = \dd t\) $$ (与式) = \int \frac{\ln t}{t} \dd t \\ $$ さらに、\(\ln t = u\)とおくと、\(\frac{1}{t} \dd t = \dd u\) $$ \begin{aligned} (与式) &= \int u \dd u \\ &= \frac{1}{2} u^2 + C \\ &= \frac{1}{2} (\ln t)^2 + C \\ &= \frac{1}{2} (\ln \sin x)^2 + C \, (Cは積分定数) \end{aligned} $$
